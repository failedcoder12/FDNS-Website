About F.D.N.S.

We are going to start a new unofficial technical club to help student to make projects or get some taste of team work. We are those who don't got selected in any of the technical club in NITK. But our urge to be such club help us to initiate this new thought. We have three groups F-Coders , D-Mech , N-Electro , S-Art . They all are SIG's.

About F and D

F-Coders (Failed Coder) our group will focus on Computer Engineering related stuffs . It will mainly focus on projects related to Development, Networking, Machine Learning.D-Mech (The Mechanical) our group will focus on Mechanical Engineering related stuffs . It will mainly focus on projects related to Autocad, Machines, Matlab, Robotics.

About N and S

N-Electro (New Electronics) our group will focus on EE and EC related stuffs . It will mainly focus on projects related to Matlab, Signal Processing, Circuits, Robotics etc.S-Art (Smart Art) our group will focus on Designing and Writting . It will mainly focus on Writing for webpages, facebook page, photography.
